SDG - 3 & Indian Traditions


Yoga and Meditation

Practising yoga and meditation can improve physical and mental health. These ancient Indian traditions promote relaxation, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being.

Ayurveda

Learning about Ayurveda, the traditional Indian system of medicine, can help teenagers understand the importance of holistic health and natural remedies.

Healthy Diet

Embrace traditional Indian dietary practices that include a variety of fruits, vegetables, and spices. Avoid processed foods and fast food, and maintain a balanced diet.

Physical Activity

Engage in traditional Indian physical activities like playing sports, practising martial arts (e.g., Kalari), or taking up dance forms like Bharatanatyam or Kathak.

Cultural Fitness

Participate in traditional dance and music forms that provide both physical exercise and cultural enrichment.

Herbal Remedies

Learn about the benefits of herbs and natural remedies used in traditional Indian medicine for common ailments and minor health issues.

Fasting

Explore traditional fasting practices like intermittent fasting or Ekadashi fasting, which can promote better digestion and detoxification when done in a healthy manner.

Community Health

Volunteer in community health programs and initiatives that provide healthcare services to underserved populations.

Mental Health Awareness

Raise awareness about mental health issues and the importance of seeking help when needed, breaking the stigma often associated with mental health in some Indian communities.
