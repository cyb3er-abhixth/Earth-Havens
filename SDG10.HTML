SDG - 10 & Indian Traditions

**Learn about India's Diversity**

:

Start by learning about the incredible diversity of India, including its languages, cultures, and religions. Understanding diversity is a key step in promoting inclusivity and reducing discrimination.

**Celebrate Cultural Festivals**

:

Participate in cultural festivals and events from different regions and communities within India. This can help promote cultural exchange and appreciation, fostering unity in diversity.

**Support Local Artisans**

:

Buy handicrafts and products made by local artisans and craftsmen. This supports local economies and helps reduce economic inequalities.

**Volunteer for Community Service**

:

Get involved in community service projects that aim to uplift marginalised communities. This could involve volunteering at local NGOs, schools, or community centres.

**Promote Gender Equality**

:

Support gender equality initiatives and challenge gender stereotypes. Encourage girls' education and empowerment in your community.

**Reduce Food Waste**

:

Practise mindful consumption and minimise food waste. Share excess food with those in need through community programs or shelters.
