SDG - 11 & Indian Traditions

**Community Cleanliness**

:

Participate in or initiate community clean-up drives in your neighbourhood to keep the surroundings clean and promote a sustainable living environment.

**Water Conservation**

:

Learn about traditional Indian methods of water conservation, such as rainwater harvesting, and encourage their implementation in your area to ensure a sustainable water supply.

**Public Transport**

:

Advocate for the use of public transportation or carpooling to reduce traffic congestion, air pollution, and the carbon footprint in your city.

**Tree Planting**

:

Promote awareness of traditional Indian architectural practices that can help in building more sustainable and climate-resilient structures.

**Promote Traditional Architecture**

:

Promote awareness of traditional Indian architectural practices that can help in building more sustainable and climate-resilient structures.

**Support Local Markets**

:

Buy products from local artisans and markets to promote sustainable, locally sourced goods and help preserve traditional craftsmanship.

**Heritage Preservation**

:

Get involved in efforts to protect and restore historical monuments and heritage sites in your city, preserving cultural identity.

**Community Gardens**

:

Create or participate in community gardens or urban farming projects to promote green spaces and access to fresh produce in urban areas.
